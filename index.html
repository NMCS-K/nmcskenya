What is NMCS-K?

National Movement of Catholic Students- Kenya is a movement of tertiary Catholic students (as its name suggests) brought together for the purpose of serving God and the community. Our motto is Preferential Option for the Poor. Our aim in the community is to promote peace and serve the less fortunate in the community through our activities
